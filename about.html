Urgent Precision in Healthcare Logistics.

VitalTrack BD is a high-stakes data routing system designed to alleviate the critical bottlenecks in emergency bed allocation across Bangladesh. By synthesizing live telemetry from distributed healthcare facilities, we provide a hyper-legible, real-time operating picture for first responders and patients in distress.
